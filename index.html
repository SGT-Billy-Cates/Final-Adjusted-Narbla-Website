About NARBLA

The purpose of this regional association shall be:

  • Cooperate with the Academy of Legal Studies in Business (ALSB).
  • Promote scholarship and superior teaching of Business Law in colleges and universities.
  • Foster understanding between administrators and teachers of Business Law.
  • Encourage professional networking among teachers and scholars.
  • Promote high ethical standards in business.
  • Provide a medium for exchanging information about teaching, opportunities, and legal developments in the region.
  • Host meetings for social and professional purposes and problem-solving discussions.

Business Law Review

The Official Publication of the North Atlantic Regional Business Law Association (NARBLA) – www.narbla.org

The Business Law Review is published annually to encourage scholarly research in Business Law and the Legal Environment of Business.
